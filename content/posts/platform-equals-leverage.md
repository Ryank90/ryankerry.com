I have lost count of how many times I have heard someone in a leadership meeting refer to platform engineering as “the internal IT team” or “a necessary cost.” It is frustrating—but I get it. When you are not close to the work, it is easy to think of platforms as plumbing: useful, but invisible, and not something that moves the business forward.
